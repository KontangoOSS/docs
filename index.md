# Kontango Documentation

Open-source platform for running your own cloud on hardware you own. Use what you have, host where it makes sense, pay for help when you want it, do it yourself when you don't.

## Start here

If you're new, the [Concepts](concepts/) section explains what Kontango is in plain language. Eight short pages, each answers one question.

- **[What is Kontango?](concepts/what-is-kontango)** — One paragraph, three examples
- **[The network](concepts/the-network)** — What "the Kontango network" means
- **[Enrolling a device](concepts/enrollment)** — What happens when you add your laptop
- **[Identities](concepts/identities)** — What "identity" means for a device or service
- **[Services](concepts/services)** — What you actually connect to
- **[Policy](concepts/policy)** — Who is allowed to talk to what
- **[When things break](concepts/when-things-break)** — Symptoms and fixes
- **[Getting help](concepts/getting-help)** — Email, Discord, paid support
