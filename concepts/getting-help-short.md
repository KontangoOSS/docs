# Getting Help

Three ways to reach us:

- **[Discord](https://discord.gg/CrXWJfwD)** — fastest. Casual questions, community help.
- **`hello@kontango.io`** — general questions, partnerships, "is this right for me?"
- **`support@kontango.io`** — real issues. Broken enrollments, services down, security concerns.

Kontango is open source and free. Help is paid work, the same way RedHat sells support on free software. You can run everything yourself, or pay us to deploy and manage it. Both paths are real.

When you email, include: what you were doing, what happened, when it started, your device's hostname, and your OS. The more detail, the faster we can help.

Switch to [medium](?v=medium) for the email template, or [long](?v=long) for response-time expectations and what to include in different situations.
