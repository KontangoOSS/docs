# What Is Kontango?

Kontango is open-source software for running your own private cloud — on whatever hardware you already own.

Instead of renting storage, code repositories, dashboards, and login systems from different SaaS companies, you run all of those things yourself, on a Raspberry Pi or an old desktop or a small server in a closet. Kontango handles the hard parts: connecting your devices privately, encrypting traffic, deciding who's allowed to reach what.

**The point:** your data stays on hardware you own, in a place you choose, without anyone else's terms of service. The whole platform is free. Paid help is available if you want it.

**Three things you do with it:**

1. Open `git.tango` in your browser from your couch and reach your private code
2. Run a small business's whole tech stack on one server in the back office
3. Build a homelab where your phone and laptop both reach internal services without opening any ports
